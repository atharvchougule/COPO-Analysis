About Us

Our Mission

At Course Compass, we are dedicated to empowering educators with innovative tools to enhance the quality of education. Our mission is to simplify the process of calculating and analyzing Course Outcomes (CO) and Program Outcomes (PO), providing educators with valuable insights to drive curriculum improvement and academic planning.

Our Vision

We envision a future where educational institutions can seamlessly integrate data-driven decision-making into their teaching practices. By providing user-friendly, efficient, and reliable tools, we aim to support educators in delivering high-quality education that meets the evolving needs of students and the academic community.

What We Do

Our CO/PO Analysis Web App is designed to assist educators in:

  • Calculating and Analyzing Outcomes: Streamlining the process of evaluating Course and Program Outcomes using data collected from Excel spreadsheets.
  • Real-Time Feedback: Offering real-time insights and feedback to help educators make informed decisions.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Providing a simple, intuitive interface through Material UI, making it easy for educators to input data and generate comprehensive reports.
  • Optimized Data Handling: Utilizing Microsoft Excel for efficient data storage and operations, ensuring optimal performance and ease of use.

Our Team

We are a team of dedicated developers, educators, and data analysts who are passionate about leveraging technology to enhance educational outcomes. Our diverse backgrounds and expertise allow us to create solutions that are both practical and impactful.

Why Choose Us

  • Innovative Solutions: We combine cutting-edge technology with a deep understanding of educational needs.
  • Ease of Use: Our focus on user experience ensures that our tools are accessible and easy to use for educators at all levels.
  • Continuous Improvement: We are committed to continually improving our application based on user feedback and the latest educational research.
